Introduction

Total United was established in 1954 by Abdul Latif F. Al Thuwainy and Nagib Ibrahim Najjar, two ambitious entrepreneurs with the strong belief that building projects start with building relationships.Since then, AHMADIAH has never ceased to grow, hence becoming Kuwait’s leading name in the civil engineering and building construction industry. Adherence to very high standards of service, commitment to timely delivery, valuing good client relationship and taking pride in its human capital and it’s culture enabled AHMADIAH to maintain its distinctive reputation as evidenced by its impressive track record in the execution of fast track projects and design, design development & built projects (DDDB).The most prestigious organisations in the region and abroad have come to rely on AHMADIAH’s complete range of specialist skills in civil and industrial sectors; AHMADIAH is highly reputed for the level of experience of its management, which is supported by highly qualified professionals in all fields of construction.In 1994, the company became a closed shareholding.Today AHMADIAH achievements have been recognised and rewarded by the large number of its repeat clients which puts it in a class of it’s own.On the trading side, our subsidiaries and sister companies add substantial value to our services.The commitment of AHMADIAH is to remain “The Leader” in the field, be capable of responding to its clients’ needs and gain their full satisfaction.
